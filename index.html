शुभ दीपावली



हि दीपावली खूप आनंदमयी आणि आरोग्यदायी जावो .
आमच्याकडून दीपावली च्या हार्दिक शुभेच्छा!



On the auspicious day of Diwali
Wishing you success,
Happiness and Prosperity.
Happy Diwali




Sun glows for a day, Candle for an hour,
Matchstick for a minute,
But a wish can glow days forever,
So here is my wish for glowing Diwali,
glowing life!!
